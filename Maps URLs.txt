Google Maps API -- Maps URLs
https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/urls

1.Search
https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query={place name}(address)

2.Direction (交通方式為開車)
https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin={place name}&destination={place name}&travelmode=driving

若有經過地點，多個連接地點加(|) 例如：五權車站|台中火車站
https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin={place name}&waypoints={place name}&destination={place name}&travelmode=driving
